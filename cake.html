Rosette Cake Competition

Bake your finest rosette-inspired cake and bring it along for judging. Prizes will be awarded in each age category.

Enter Your Cake

Bring your rosette cake to the judging table before 11:30am.

Entries will be judged by age category.

Winners announced at the afternoon prize presentation.

Need to Know More?

Bring your completed rosette cake to the Rosette Cake Tent before judging begins. A steward will record your name and age category before placing your entry for display.
The competition includes Junior, Youth, and Adult categories, so bakers of all ages can take part.
Winners will be announced during the afternoon prize presentation at 1:30pm.
