Rosette Cake Competition

Bake your finest rosette-inspired cake and bring it along for judging. Prizes will be awarded in each age category.

Competition Rules

Bring your finest rosette-inspired cake to enter the competition.

Entries must be homemade and presented before judging begins.

Cakes will be judged within their age category.

Prizes will be awarded for the best rosette cake in each category.

Please label your cake clearly with the baker’s name and age group.

All cakes should remain untouched until judging is complete.
