Rosette Cake Competition

Bake your finest rosette-inspired cake and bring it along for judging. Prizes will be awarded in each age category.

Competition Rules

Purchase a QR entry to submit your guess.

Enter your estimate for the full weight of the rosette cake.

The closest guess wins the cake.

In the event of a tie, a winner will be drawn at random.

Repeated guesses are permitted, within reason and good taste.

Please admire the cake respectfully and refrain from poking it.
