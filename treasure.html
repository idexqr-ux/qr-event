Treasure Hunt

Help find the missing rosettes

Oh dear — The Matchem Charity Hobby Horse Race can’t begin. The prize rosettes have gone missing somewhere around the village green.

How it works

Purchase your Treasure Hunt QR to receive the first clue.

Follow each clue carefully and continue to the next location.

Return your completed hunt sheet to the Prize Tent when finished.

The fastest correct entry, or a draw among correct finishers, will win the prize.

First Clue

To start your treasure journey,
Find where the helpers serve drinks on trays.

Hint: Look for somewhere with cups, cakes, and people pretending they are not queueing for seconds.
