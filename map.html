Find your way around the village green before the fun begins.

Event map description

The entrance and parking are at the bottom of the map. The Cake Tent is near the entrance for easy cake drop-off. The Parade Ring sits in the centre of the village green, with the hobby horse racecourse looping around it. The Oak Tree is also inside the racecourse loop and is one of the treasure hunt landmarks. The Refreshments Tent and Prize Tent are near the top of the green. The Start and Finish area is beside the racecourse, leading into the Willow Cottage Finish Straight.
